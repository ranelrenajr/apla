Awards Process

1

Nomination/Invitation

Potential nominations can be self-applied or sent by another person/party. Nomination forms can be requested from the MPJ Entertainment Productions secretariat. An initial assessment shall be done by the secretariat and if the nominees fall into the different award categories and possess the minimum criteria an official nomination form with instructions shall be given.

2

Nomination Period

A period of submission of nominations shall be announced by the MPJ secretariat and only the nominations that are submitted and completed shall be considered.

3

Shortlisting

A panel of judges shall be organized by both award giving bodies to start the shortlisting process. The nominations that are duly and completely answered with complete documents shall be included in the selection by the panel of judges.

4

Announcement

The selected Luminaries will be informed thru letter of their nomination to the award and shall be invited to the awards night. The nominee should confirm attendance, or must inform awards committee if a representative will claim the award.

5

Awards Night

The trophy/plaque and other awards shall be given at the awards night. In case the selected awardee will not be able to come nor their representative, the award committees may choose to give the award to the next potential LUMINARY.

Showbiz Awards Mechanics

1

Survey

The awards council will conduct a survey for a particular period (3 months; 6 months; 1 year) to determine which individuals, couple or company from the above-mentioned categories are trending and is creating a buzz/attention. Those that receive the top ten highest points will be the nominees for showbiz category awards. The survey will be sent to selected persons who have interest and knowledge in the various categories.  

2

Judging

50% of the total score for each shortlisted nominee will come from the deliberation of a panel of judges chosen for their knowledge and experience/exposure on the related category. at least 3 judges will compose the panel. The other 50% will come from votes from the public. The award council will reveal the nominees to the public through social media and will provide the mechanics on how to vote for their favorite individuals, pair or company. At the end of the voting period, the scores from the judges and the votes will be combined and will be used to determine the winners. winners will be invited to the awards night. Since their are many categories in the showbiz awards, a separate awards night maybe conducted.
