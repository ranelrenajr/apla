What is Asia Pacific
Luminare Awards

            There are many stars in the sky, some shine brighter than others. But one thing is true, each one is a star regardless of the level of brightness or shine. Likewise, there are many people in our world who contribute to the wellness and welfare of our society. Most of the time, it is only those who make the most contribution, those who shine brightest, that get to be recognized and celebrated. In Asia Pacific LUMINARE Awards and Asia's Top Luminary Awards, we believe that there are people that contribute to the enrichment of lives of others and it is their time to shine!

            Both the Asia Pacific LUMINARE Awards and Asia's Top Luminary Awards would like to honor and recognize them for their contribution and accomplishments. It is essential that we learn from their experiences and encourage them to continue in what they are doing and even support their growth so that they can be more and do more.

            Recipients of the awards are selected from candidates nominated by the awards council (peers from the same field, or, beneficiaries of their work). Each month a particular field is highlighted and nominees are received two (2) months prior to the awarding to give time for the award council to gather and confirm information provided.

Our Beginnings

Our Beginnings With the goal of inspiring college students from different universities in the Philippines, who have excelled in their academics, the first Asia Pacific Luminare was conducted in 2018 held at Robinsons Place in Las Pinas. The first Asia's Top Luminary Awards was held that same year at Saigon Central hotel, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, honoring excellent students and teachers from Cambodia, Thailand, Philippines and the host country.

With the success of the first two awardings in the Philippines by Asia Pacific Luminare Awads and the first Asia's Top Luminary Awards in Vietnam, we would like to expand by honoring/recognizing other fields like public service, governance, business, private sector, education, fashion, technology, environment, faith-based groups, charity, Entertainment, Arts and many other categories whose work is making our world, at least in the Asia Pacific Region, a better place.
